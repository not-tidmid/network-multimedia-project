Our Foundation

Blissful Bakery was founded by owner’s Austin Moore, Kayla Liberus, and Diamond Deas. They are all student’s at Florida A&M University who found comfort in sweets during their most stressful time of the semester, exam season. One year they decided to start baking all kinds of delicious sweets to help them make it through a stressful exam season. Today, Blissful Bites is the hottest commodity on the Florida A&M University campus where students from all over come to indulge in the delicious treats to make it through the most crucial time of their academic careers.

The Secret ingredients

Three of our secret ingredients and what we consider to be “fan favorites” are our famous raspberry jam, cookie dough, and cream cheese fillings. We feel that these amazing fillings pair perfectly with our signature strawberry, cookies and cream, and vanilla cupcakes and we know our customers couldn’t agree more! These fillings are our fans greatest addition to a perfectly delicious cupcake.
